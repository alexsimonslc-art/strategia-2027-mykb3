Loyola College

An Institution of Excellence

Established in 1925 by the Society of Jesus (Jesuits), Loyola College in Chennai stands as a beacon of academic prestige and holistic development in India. Renowned for its rigorous training, exceptional faculty, and outstanding campus placement, Loyola is consistently ranked among the top collegiate institutions nationally.

With a vision to form credible global leaders who possess creative intellect and social commitment, Loyola fosters an environment where innovation thrives, preparing students to lead the professional world with character.

The Department

B.Com. Honours Program

The Department of Commerce (B.Com. Honours) at Loyola College represents the peak of undergraduate business education. Known for its highly specialized syllabus, rigorous research requirements, and exclusive industrial internships, this elite division prepares candidates for complex strategic corporate roles, professional certifications (like CA, ACCA), and higher education.

Through intensive case studies, masterclasses, and specialized seminars, the Honours stream nurtures a highly collaborative and intellectually competitive cohort.

The Event

Strategia 2027

Strategia is the flagship national-level symposium crafted by the B.Com. Honours students of Loyola. Acting as the ultimate test of strategic wisdom, leadership acumen, and financial intellect, the event brings together the sharpest undergraduate minds from premier universities across India.

On 25th December 2027, the battlegrounds will open for high-stakes competition. Under pressure, with live scenarios and realistic corporate simulations, only the best minds will walk away with the legendary title of Strategia Champion.

STRATEGIA2027

Annual National Symposium organized by the Department of Commerce (B.Com. Honours), Loyola College, Chennai.
